How Did ___ Vote?

Congress is a very unpopular institution among Americans. Year after year, when polled, the majority of Americans consistently disapprove of Congress. In an era of increasing partianship and political polarization, it is important to stay informed on politics and to form your own opinions. Too often people let their political opinions be formed by those around them without doing their own research. The goal of this website is to help you be able to easily search the voting records of any member of Congress so that you can examine their records for yourself, read the bill for yourself, and most importantly to form your opinions yourself. If you would like to see how your own Congressional representative voted, then you can click here to find your district number if you don't know it.
